Challenge

The Problem

Manual review of 15+ different financial document types was slow, inconsistent, and lacked audit trails for risk decisions.

  • —4+ hours manual review time per application
  • —Inconsistent risk assessments across analysts
  • —Lack of standardized audit trails for decisions

Solution

The Approach

Built a RAG pipeline utilizing GPT-4 and Pinecone to retrieve relevant context and generate cited risk assessments from unstructured documents.

01

Implemented semantic chunking for financial documents

02

Created vector index with Pinecone for fast retrieval

03

Engineered prompts with citation requirements for explainability

04

Built FastAPI service for real-time document analysis

Decisions

Why RAG?

Regulatory requirement for explainability. RAG provides exact source citations for every risk factor identified.

Why Pinecone?

Managed service offered best-in-class latency (<50ms) and scalability without maintenance overhead.

Architecture

Document Upload → Text Extraction → Chunking & Embedding → Pinecone Index → GPT-4 Querying → Risk Report Generation

Outcome

  • ✓Reduced analysis time from 4 hours to 3 seconds
  • ✓Achieved 87% accuracy in risk factor identification
  • ✓Standardized risk assessment across the organization
  • ✓Enabled full auditability with source citations
